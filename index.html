About Ashley

Ashley is a performing arts teacher and music director based in the Bay Area.

Ashley holds a B.M. in Music Theater from Baldwin Wallace Conservatory of Music, and has more than 20 years of experience working with voice, piano, and acting. She has performed in regional theater productions in the Cleveland and Philadelphia areas, with companies such as Great Lakes Theater Company, Cleveland Public Theater, Bucks County Playhouse, and The Beck Center for The Arts. Favorite roles include Belle ("Beauty & the Beast"), Nina ("The Seagull"), Laurie ("Oklahoma"), and Serena ("Legally Blonde").

In addition to her stage experience, Ashley has been featured in national and regional commercials and short films. Her original songs have been featured in songwriting competitions in Cleveland and Philadelphia.

Ashley served as the in-house music director for several productions at The Limelight Performing Arts Center in West Chester, PA, favorites productions including "Matilda" and "Annie". She currently teaches elementary music at Resurrection School in Sunnyvale and serves on the board of the San Francisco Bay Area Chapter of NATS (The National Association of Teachers of Singing) as VP of Recitals.

Piano

Beginner lessons for all ages

Piano lessons are offered as 30- or 60- minute sessions. Available in person at my home studio, or online via Zoom.

  • Classical piano training for beginners using Alfred's method (books required)
  • Major and minor scales & chords
  • Focus on music theory and rhythm
  • Ear training
  • Sight-reading
  • Repertoire diversification and expansion

Voice

For all ages and levels

Voice lessons are offered as 30- or 60- minute sessions. Available in person at my home studio, or online via Zoom

  • Musical Theater, Pop, Classical, and Singer/Songwriter vocal technique (belt, mix, legit soprano, falsetto)
  • College audition preparation and coaching
  • Audition book build-out
  • Repertoire development and diversification
  • Two annual recital opportunities
  • Customized piano backing tracks and transpositions

Acting

For all ages and levels

Acting lessons are offered as 30- or 60- minute sessions. Available in person at my home studio, or online via Zoom.

  • Audition monologue prep
  • Theater scene study
  • Film scene study
  • Commercial scene study
  • Vocal projection and technique
  • Utilization of Alexander technique for mind/body emotional connection
